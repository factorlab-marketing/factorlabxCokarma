Quick Poll #2

If Markets Fall 20%, You Would...

How do you react to volatility?

A

Pause SIP

"I'll wait for the market to recover before investing more."

B

Withdraw

"I need to stop the bleeding and book my losses now."

Best Strategy
C

Continue SIP

"I'll buy more units at lower prices (accumulate)."

Why C is the Winner: Rupee Cost Averaging

When markets fall, your SIP amount buys more units. When markets recover, these extra units multiply your gains. FactorLab portfolios are built to help you stay disciplined and capture this advantage automatically.
